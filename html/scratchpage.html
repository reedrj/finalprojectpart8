Why Do I Like Dunking?

I love doing things that are hard and challenging. I also love seeing progress with how my body functions. All growing up I loved doing everything that revolved around sports and things that were active. My Sophomore year college coaches, for football, were telling me that I was too small. That motived me to prove them wrong because I loved when people would doubt me. Usually in dunking, the taller you are the easier it is. Therefore, I wanted to prove to everyone that size did not matter. From my hard work, I gave other kids confidence that they can succeed, even when they are not particularlly bigger.

  1. Reasons I Love Dunking:
    • It keeps me active and in shape.
    • It motivates younger kids to work hard towards goals.
    • It's a great way to have a goal and work towards a goal you want to achieve.
    • It bring me joy seeing progress in something I am passionate about.

  2. Types of Dunks I've Accomplished:
    • Windmill Dunk Off Of Lob
    • 360 Dunk
    • 360 Two Handed Dunk
    • Windmill Dunk Off Of The Dribble
    • Two Handed Alley-Oop Dunk
    • Off The Backboard Two Handed Dunk
